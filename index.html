Levey's Triumph: From Starvation to Strength

Found starving and abandoned, Levey was near death. Thanks to donations, Stray Rescue of St. Louis provided critical care, including life-saving treatment and gradual refeeding. Once skeletal, Levey now thrives, displaying her playful spirit. Her recovery demonstrates the direct impact of generosity, offering her a hopeful future.
